## Option B — Short thread (4 tweets)

**1/**
Most DAO payroll tools do one of two things: pay everyone the same flat
amount, or make an admin manually decide who gets what.

Both are broken.

So I built ReputationVault — pay that's actually earned. 🧵

**2/**
How it works:

Deposits auto-split into 3 pools the moment they land:
→ 70% contributor pool
→ 20% operational reserve
→ 10% locked emergency fund

No manual routing. No admin moving money around by hand.

**3/**
Contributors earn reputation when milestones get logged. Go quiet, your
score fades. Ship, it grows.

Every ~month, the contributor pool pays out automatically — split by
reputation, not seniority or a fixed number.

Anyone can trigger it. No one has to remember payroll.

**4/**
The emergency fund has real teeth: releasing it needs multiple trusted
signers AND a time-lock. Not even the admin can move it alone.
